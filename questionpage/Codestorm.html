Welcome to Kohli’s Reverse Reflex Test. You are controlling a batsman facing a mystery bowler. Balls will be hurled at random speeds. Press the right key just before the ball reaches the batsman to hit it. Your job: Score as many hits as possible in 10 balls. At the end, Kohli will judge your performance, and your stats will be logged in a secret vault (MySQL database).

A pop up appears 0.2 secs telling which key to press before ball hits the stumps Keys are of your choice 6. Track number of hits and total balls faced. 7.
